Food

Conference dinner for the speakers (mandatory registration by 2 December) will be served on Friday at 20.00 at Gostilnica 5-6 kg.
There will be catering lunches served at the venues for speakers during lunch breaks, while others can find many options in the immediate vicinity of the faculties. Just a short walk from the Faculty of arts, you’ll discover some restaurants and fast food spots to check out: Zbornica bar in žar, Burek Olimpija, Picaboj, Das ist Valter. They are all located on Rimska street, as well as other food stops.
The same goes for the Faculty of social sciences, where several options for lunch or snacks are also nearby: Roza slon, Tiskarna, Barbado, Slovely.

City transportation

To use public buses, you need an Urbana card (available at kiosks, post-offices) or Urbana Mobile App. Without Urbana, you can also pay a 90-minute ride with a payment card. You can also use a taxi. Two most popular taxi companies are Metro & Rondo. You can call them, order them via mobile app or online.

Clubs

The area of Metelkova City at Metelkova 10 near the train station hosts a variety of clubs with different profiles, DJs collectives, and events. Notable venues include Klub Monokel, Channel Zero, Klub Tiffany, Gala hala, Klub Gromka. Another significant location is Kersnikova 4, where a renowned K4 club and the Zorica bar are situated. Popular clubbing destinations also include Publika Bar Klub at Vilharjeva cesta 11, or Cirkus. You can also visit the recently squated venue Plac at Linhartova cesta 43.
